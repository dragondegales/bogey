# Privacy Policy

Bogey does not collect personal data.

## What we collect

Nothing.

## What is stored on your device

Your scores, your course selection, and your player names if you have set them. All of this is stored locally on your Apple Watch in the app's private storage directory. It is not accessible to other apps.

## What is stored in iCloud

Nothing. Bogey does not use iCloud or CloudKit. Your data does not leave your watch unless you explicitly export it.

## What is shared with third parties

Nothing.

## Analytics

None. The app contains no analytics SDK.

## Crash reporting

Bogey does not include a crash reporting SDK. If you install the app through the App Store or TestFlight, Apple may collect opt-in crash reports through its standard platform process. That data goes to Apple, not to us, and is subject to Apple's privacy policy.

## Network requests

The app makes three types of network requests, all to an API we operate:

**Course catalog.** On launch, the app fetches a list of golf courses. This is a standard HTTP request containing no user identifiers. No account is required.

**Round export.** The settings screen includes an optional feature to export your round data. Using it sends your scores to our API, associated with an email address you enter manually. This is opt-in and user-initiated. We use it to send you your round in JSON format and do not share it.

**Missing course report.** If your course is not in the catalog, you can report your GPS location so we can add it. This is opt-in and user-initiated. It sends your device's GPS coordinates and, optionally, the email address you have entered.
